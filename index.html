About Us

T E Electrical Repairs has been operating for 10+ years across Bloemfontein and the Free State. We specialise in refrigeration, air conditioning, freezer and cold room repairs, installations and preventative maintenance. Our team of certified technicians have delivered reliable solutions for domestic, retail and industrial clients.

We focus on fast diagnostics, honest quotations and long-term equipment care plans so your systems run efficiently and reliably.
